About company

Hi all. the company is with you ... For 20 years we have been importing and repairing cars from Europe and the USA for our customers on order. And so, 5 years ago, we thought about what if life could be simplified for people with rights, even for those who do not have a car.


Thus, the idea was born to create the possibility of renting cars that, for some reason, were not bought, but were brought, from class C to luxury cars. Graduation, wedding, just meeting with classmates - it doesn't matter. The main thing is that with us you can surprise anyone

Rules for rent

We have created a unique offer on the market. Anyone who has a license can rent any car from our fleet. To do this, you just need to come to us with the rights, fill out the documents with the manager and voila, the car is yours for the selected time


Car rental starts at $100 a day for C-class cars and goes up to $2,000 for LUX or SPORT cars. In our fleet you will be able to pick up a car according to your needs, and we will do our best to make you enjoy our "Car For Rent" service. See you soon, we are waiting for you.
